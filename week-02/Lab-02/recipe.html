Chocolate Milk

A Sweet and Refreshing Drink

Welcome for anyone who likes chocolate milk!

Let's make it together!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1-2 tablespoons of chocolate powder
  • 1-2 teaspoons of sugar
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Pour milk into a glass
  2. Add chocolate powder
  3. Stir well until fully mixed
  4. Add sugar if you want it sweeter
  5. Add ice cubes
  6. Add decoration if you want

Nutrition Information

Nutrient Amount
Calories 200 kcal
Protein 10g
Sugar 30g
Fat 5g
